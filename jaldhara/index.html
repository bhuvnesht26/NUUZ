Who We Are ?

Jaldhara Purifiers is a water purification company that installs state of the art R O systems to ensure clean and safe water for your daily use including drinking, cooking and other uses. Our aim is to purify water by reducing the concentration of particulate matter including suspended particles, parasites, bacteria, algae, viruses, fungi, as well as reducing the concentration of a range of dissolved and particulate matter.

We do this by installing Jaldhara R O water purification systems that remove the salt and other effluent materials from the water molecules to provide you with high nutritional water.

What is Reverse Osmosis (RO) ?

Reverse osmosis (RO) is a water purification technology that uses a semipermeable membrane to remove ions, molecules and larger particles from drinking water.
